┃                     Questions for Your CNS Facilitator                     ┃
┃                                                                            ┃
┃ When preparing to meet with your CNS facilitator, it's beneficial to have  ┃
┃ a clear set of questions that will help clarify your understanding and     ┃
┃ guide your project effectively. Here are some questions you might          ┃
┃ consider:                                                                  ┃
┃                                                                            ┃
┃  1 Clarification on PICOT:                                                 ┃
┃     • Can you provide examples of well-structured PICOT questions?         ┃
┃     • What are the common pitfalls to avoid when formulating a PICOT       ┃
┃       statement?                                                           ┃
┃  2 Selecting a Topic:                                                      ┃
┃     • What are the most critical areas of focus for improvement projects   ┃
┃       in our institution?                                                  ┃
┃     • How can I ensure my selected topic aligns with hospital priorities   ┃
┃       and patient needs?                                                   ┃
┃  3 Gathering Evidence:                                                     ┃
┃     • Could you recommend effective databases or resources for conducting  ┃
┃       the literature search?                                               ┃
┃     • Are there particular articles or guidelines that you think are vital ┃
┃       for our topic area?                                                  ┃
┃  4 Stakeholder Engagement:                                                 ┃
┃     • Who are the key stakeholders I should consider involving in this     ┃
┃       project?                                                             ┃
┃     • How can I effectively engage the Nurse Manager and other departments ┃
┃       in my intervention planning?                                         ┃
┃  5 Project Timeline:                                                       ┃
┃     • What are your suggestions for staying on track with the project      ┃
┃       timeline?                                                            ┃
┃     • How should I prioritize tasks from month to month?                   ┃
┃  6 Measuring Success:                                                      ┃
┃     • What criteria or metrics should I use to define success for my       ┃
┃       project?                                                             ┃
┃     • How can I effectively measure the impact before and after my         ┃
┃       intervention?                                                        ┃
┃  7 General Advice:                                                         ┃
┃     • What strategies do you recommend for overcoming challenges during    ┃
┃       the project?                                                         ┃
┃     • How can I best present my findings and recommendations at the end of ┃
┃       the project?                                                         ┃
┃                                                                            ┃
┃                    Tips for Finalizing Your Questions:                     ┃
┃                                                                            ┃
┃  • Tailor Questions to Your Interests: Adjust these questions based on     ┃
┃    your interests and preliminary thoughts on the project.                 ┃
┃  • Prioritize: Consider ranking these questions based on importance to     ┃
┃    your project for an effective discussion.                               ┃
┃  • Be Open to Additional Suggestions: Allow room for your CNS facilitator  ┃
┃    to offer additional insights or questions that you hadn’t considered.   ┃
┃                                                                            ┃
┃ Having thoughtful questions will help you engage in a meaningful           ┃
┃ conversation and enhance your project's development. Good luck!            ┃
